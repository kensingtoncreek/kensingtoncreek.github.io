    Pool Season Open

    posted Jul 9, 2011, 11:06 AM by Kensington Creek Manor Admin

    Just A Reminder: 
    The Pool Closes at 9pm,
    The Pool Rules are Posted at the entrance of Pool; Please read and assist in enforcing these rules so that all may continue to enjoy the pool!
    NO Alcohol allowed at pool.  Though there has been leniency, please do not abuse this.  There is absolutely NO GLASS BOTTLES allowed at pool.  This has cost us money in the past, so this will be strictly enforced.  Our community has grown quite a bit in the last year, Safety should be top priority; so it is ever important to adhere to the limit of guests, etc.
    Last, but not least, please be sure to clean up behind yourself.  Whether in restroom, or common areas of pool.  If you have any questions, please feel free to contact Liberty Community Management/Robin McKay at
    770-466-6331.

    Committees

    Pool Committee

    •         Determine Opening and Closing dates for the pool.

    •         Cleaning the bathrooms and pool area

    •         Check and replenish toilet paper, hand soap, First aid kit, fire extinguisher, etc.

    •         Take the trash out on Tuesdays – return bin to interior of pool on Wednesday

    Special Meeting Home Owners Association

    posted Apr 21, 2010, 6:28 PM by Kensington Creek Manor Admin   [ updated Apr 21, 2010, 6:47 PM ]

    A special meeting of the Kensington Creek Homeowners Association has been scheduled for:

    When: Tuesday, May 18,2010
    Time: 7:30 pm
    Where: Dacula High School

    The purpose of this meeting will be to elect the 2010 Advisory Board for the Community.

    If you are interested in serving your community on the Advisory Board send your reason for wanting to serve, your vision for the community and your qualifications to
    robin.mckay@libertycm.com or fax: 678-990-9190 or by mail to:

    LIBERTY COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT
    ATTENTION: ROBIN MCKAY
    P.O. BOX 2082 .
    LOGANVILLE, GA 30052

    *If you are unable to attend the meeting please send your proxy with another member of the community or fax/mail it to Liberty by May 18, 2010. The proxy will count towards the Quorum allowing the community to conduct business at this meeting. The individual you assign your proxy to will be able to vote on your behalf.
